<p class="heading">Tongue the notes, don't "huff and puff."</p>
<li>Start each note with your tongue by whispering the word "too." "Too Too Too Too"</li>
<li>Touch the tongue to the top of your mouth behind your teeth.</li>
<li>Your tongue should not touch the recorder.</li>
<img src="graphics/tutorials/mouthPosition.png" width="208" height="192" hspace="20" />
<li>The recorder stays in front of your teeth, and only the tip goes in your mouth.</li>
